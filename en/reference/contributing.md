> *Hyday is in an intense development phase, and we value real feedback from early users. Your participation directly shapes Hyday's future. Below are the main ways to contribute.*

## Reporting Bugs

If you run into something that doesn't look right, use the "Send feedback" button in the bottom-right corner of the app:

![Feedback](https://hyday.tw/docs/screenshots/reference-contributing-1.png)

Or open an issue on[GitHub Issues](https://github.com/mukiwu/hyday/issues). To help the team locate the problem quickly, include:

- Your Hyday version (check**Settings → About**)
- Your operating system version
- Detailed steps to reproduce the error
- The difference between expected and actual behavior
- (Optional) Screenshots or a screen recording that shows the issue

## Suggesting Features

Feature ideas are welcome in the**#hyday**channel on[Discord](https://discord.com/invite/EMhdBUMuYw), or as issues on[GitHub Issues](https://github.com/mukiwu/hyday/issues)tagged with`enhancement`. Try to cover:

- **The core pain point you want to solve**: describe**what task you were doing when you got stuck**, rather than listing features directly.
- **Your current workaround**: how do you currently work around the problem?
- **The ideal experience**: what design do you think would most effectively improve your workflow?

## Content and Knowledge Sharing

If you publish posts, tutorials, or advanced tips about Hyday on your blog or social media, let us know — we'll help amplify your work through these official channels:

- The VVIP Discord community
- The official newsletter

## Contact Channels

- **Technical questions and feedback**:[GitHub Issues](https://github.com/mukiwu/hyday/issues)first, or the**#hyday**channel on[Discord](https://discord.com/invite/EMhdBUMuYw)
- **Business inquiries and VVIP consultations**:`mukispace@gmail.com`

> **ℹ️ Info**
> The most valuable feedback often comes from those**small annoyances you've quietly put up with**during everyday note-taking. The product team can't anticipate every real-world scenario, and your real experience is what helps Hyday evolve.
