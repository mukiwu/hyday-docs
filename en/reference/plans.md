## Flexible Payment Options

- **VVIP**: monthly or annual subscription, including full feature access and community perks.

> **ℹ️ Info**
> The advanced features are in final testing, and the official payment flow isn't open yet. VVIP subscriptions will launch once the system is fully stable.

## What Happens If I Cancel?

- **Data safety**: your local notes and journal entries are always preserved in full; Hyday will never restrict your access.
- **Feature access**: VVIP-only features like persona distillation and the roundtable will no longer be available.
