## How to Switch the Interface Language

Go to**Settings → General → Interface Language**and pick your preferred locale. The Hyday website and the app's top navigation bar also include a quick language switcher.

## Interface Language vs. Content Language

- **Interface language**: controls the display language of menu labels, button text, settings options, and system notifications.
- **Content language**: the language of the text you write in your notes and journal.

These two are entirely independent. For example, you can use the English interface while writing all your notes in Chinese. Hyday's search index and scanning engine do not restrict the language of note content.
