> *The first time you open Hyday, it guides you through picking a storage folder. All your notes and journal entries live there, and Hyday never uploads your note files on its own.*

## Launch Options

- **Choose a Storage Location**: pick an existing folder, or create a new one to hold your notes and journal entries
- **Import from Obsidian**: bring notes, journal entries, images, and other data from Obsidian into Hyday.
- **Start Fresh**: begin with an empty folder and create new notes and journal entries from scratch.
- **Load the Sample Folder**: comes with a complete set of example content so you can get up to speed with the core features in about 5 minutes.

![The first-launch screen showing three side-by-side options](https://hyday.tw/docs/screenshots/start-first-launch-1.png)

## Signing In Is Optional

Hyday**works fully without signing in**. Sign-in is only used for:

- Syncing a few personal settings (newsletter subscription, future dashboard layout, VVIP status verification).
- Purchasing VVIP requires a Google sign-in to verify your subscription status.

Your note content always stays in the folder you chose and**is never uploaded to a cloud server**.

## Toggle Light / Dark Mode

Click the**theme toggle**button in the top-right corner, or open the Command Palette (`cmd(ctrl)`+`Shift`+`P`) and search for**Toggle theme**. By default, the theme follows your operating system's appearance setting.

> **ℹ️ Info**
> If you're not sure whether to sign in, just skip it. All features work without an account, and you can always sign in later from the**Settings**page.
