> * The Bookshelf lets you bring your local EPUB books into Hyday to read, highlight, and take notes — and even discuss them with the AI personas you've distilled. The Bookshelf is a VVIP-only feature. *

## Put your books on the shelf

 Point Hyday at a folder in settings and it scans every EPUB beneath it (including subfolders) into your library. Book files and reading progress stay on your machine and are never uploaded. 

## Built-in reader

- **Paging & layout**: adjust the font and theme (including an eye-care / night mode) for comfortable long reading sessions.
- **AI bilingual translation**: translate a passage on the spot, with the original and translation shown side by side as you read.

## Highlights & notes

Mark what matters and jot down your thoughts:

- **Color highlights & comments**: mark passages in different colors and add your own comments beside them.
- **Cross-book search**: search the highlights and notes across all of your books at once.
- **Export to Hyday notes**: turn highlights and notes into Hyday notes so they reconnect to your backlinks, tags, and entities.

## Discuss with your distilled personas

 Pick a highlight and invite a [distilled AI persona](https://hyday.tw/docs/concepts/distillation) to discuss that passage with you, extending your thinking from a different angle — like chatting with a friend who has read the book. 

> **ℹ️ Info**
>  Book files, reading progress, and highlights all stay on your device. Only the passage you select is sent to your configured AI provider when you use translation or discussion. If that's a concern, set your provider to a local Ollama instance. 

> **ℹ️ Info**
>  The Bookshelf is a VVIP-only feature. The sidebar "Bookshelf" entry is visible to everyone; opening it without a subscription shows a feature introduction. See [Plans](https://hyday.tw/docs/reference/plans).
