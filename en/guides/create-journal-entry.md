> *Journal entries capture the moments of your day. Hyday automatically creates a new entry named after the date each day.*

## 1. Open Today's Journal

Click**Journal**in the sidebar. If today's entry doesn't exist yet, Hyday creates a`YYYY-MM-DD.md`file (for example,`2026-05-09.md`) automatically.

## 3. Advanced: Organize with**Blocks**

Each new paragraph is treated as a standalone**block**by Hyday. You can drag blocks around at any time to reorder your entry.

## 5. Browse and Search Past Entries

Click any date under "Activity" in the Journal sidebar tab, or use global search (`⌘`+`K`) and type a date to jump straight to a specific day's entry.

> **ℹ️ Info**
> Three quick lines or a few thousand words — both count as a journal entry. What matters is the habit of**capturing something the same day**. The fragments become surprisingly valuable when you look back later.
