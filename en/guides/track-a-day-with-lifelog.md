## Advanced: Combine Entities and Tags

Adding entities and tags to the text next to a mark sharpens lineage analysis:

```
`-(14:30) Met with @Alice to talk through [[Q3 Execution Plan]] #work`
```

> **ℹ️ Info**
> Hyday isn't trying to track how busy you are. The more detail you capture, the more insight self-reviews and AI lineage analysis can surface later.
